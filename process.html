What makes the QR system work

QR ear tag

Unique ID per animal, laser‑etched, withstands mud, rain, and sun. Scannable with any smartphone.

Vaccination registry

Secure database storing vaccine batch, date, and next booster. Automatically flags overdue animals.

Daily health check

Farmer scans and answers 5 simple health questions. Machine learning flags unusual patterns.

Movement control

Permits are auto‑generated for FMD‑negative animals. Positive or suspect animals are blocked.

National dashboard

Real‑time view of all tagged animals, outbreak zones, and vaccination coverage by district.

Blockchain anchor

Each scan and event is hashed on Hedera, providing immutable proof for auditors and trade partners.

Three pillars of digital biosecurity

1. Unique digital identity

Every animal gets a tamper‑proof QR code that links to a permanent health record.

2. Verified vaccination

Vaccine batch, date, and vet are recorded at source – no paper, no fraud.

3. Live traceability

From farm to abattoir, every movement is scanned and geolocated.
