Critical National Infrastructure

Transforming Disease Control from Reactive to Precision Intelligence

Aligned to World Organisation for Animal Health standards, LivestockPulseSA is a dual-tech strategy designed to secure South Africa's borders, protect our farmers, and safeguard our agricultural economy.

FMD is an Economic Handbrake

Current reactive methods rely on delayed detection and blunt-force geographic lockdowns. By the time symptoms are visible, the disease has already spread, causing catastrophic loss of export credibility.

  • Delayed Detection: Disease spreads before clinical symptoms appear.
  • Data Blindspots: No digital record of animal-to-animal interaction.
  • Fragmented Systems: Manual paper permits and isolated RFID silos do not communicate.
